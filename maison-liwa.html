Heritage

House of Al Nahyan

The Al Nahyan, sheikhs of the Bani Yas, have led Abu Dhabi since the oasis of Liwa first watered the tribe. Theirs is a court of desert and sea — falconry, pearl-diving and the majlis — today expressed through Qasr Al Watan and the Louvre Abu Dhabi.

Savoir-Faire

The craft beneath the maison.

Al Sadu, the Bedouin geometric weave inscribed by UNESCO, and falconry, equally listed; the gold-thread bisht worn over the kandura. Liwa builds on these — hand-woven sadu motifs, falcon-grey wools, and bisht tailoring bordered in gold.

The Maison

What Liwa makes.

Sharp desert-modern tailoring: the bisht-coat, sadu-woven scarves, falcon-clasp leather, and an oud-and-amber scent. A palette of sand, sable and gold.
