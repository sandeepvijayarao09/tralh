Heritage

House of Grimaldi

The Grimaldi have held the Rock of Monaco since 1297 — Europe’s oldest reigning house. The Order of Saint-Charles (1858), Garnier’s Belle-Époque Monte-Carlo, and the wedding of Grace Kelly in 1956 set its glamour.

Savoir-Faire

The craft beneath the maison.

Belle-Époque gilding, the ceremonial orders of the Principality, and Riviera couture. Le Rocher builds eveningwear, gilt-and-enamel jewellery and the yacht-and-casino wardrobe.

The Maison

What Le Rocher makes.

Monte-Carlo eveningwear and Riviera tailoring; gilt jewellery, the order sash and fine evening leather. A palette of Grimaldi red, white and gold.
