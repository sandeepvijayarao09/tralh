Heritage

House of Saud

From the mudbrick walls of At-Turaif — a UNESCO World Heritage site — the House of Saud raised the first Saudi state in 1727 and unified the Kingdom in 1932. The Najdi court endures in the Ardah and the gold-bordered bisht.

Savoir-Faire

The craft beneath the maison.

The gold-embroidered bisht of Al-Ahsa, Al Sadu weaving, and Najdi door-geometry. Diriyah renders these as hand-couched gold bisht, sadu intarsia, and mud-fort silhouettes.

The Maison

What Diriyah makes.

Ceremonial bisht couture, gold-on-sable eveningwear, and architectural leather drawn from At-Turaif. A palette of ochre, sable and gold.
