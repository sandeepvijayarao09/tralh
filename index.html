About the House

One house. Twenty-one crowns.

TRALH is the parent house — the atelier, the codex and the quality bench behind a portfolio of royal maisons. It takes every idea to finished reality, one hundred per cent in-house, and maintains each piece for life. Each royal house is an independent brand, drawn from a living or historical court, offered at three levels — Sovereign, Flagship and Signature.

The Manifesto

Where others pursue novelty,
we pursue permanence.

Five articles govern everything the House makes. They do not change with the season.

Article I

Heritage is a Discipline

We do not borrow from the past; we descend from it. Every code traces to a verifiable craft lineage. Pastiche is forbidden.

Article II

Seven Maisons, One Standard

Each maison commands its craft with autonomy. None may breach the codes: palette, mark, quality, provenance, packaging.

Article III

Craft Before Calendar

We release when craft permits, not when a season demands. A piece that is not ready does not ship.

Article IV

Provenance is Our Currency

Every hide, fibre, stone, and metal has a named origin. No anonymous material enters the House.

Article V

The Wearer is the Heir

A royal piece is owned, repaired, and inherited — not consumed. Lifetime restoration on every signed piece.
