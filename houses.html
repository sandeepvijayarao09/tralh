Houses Royales

A house for every crown.

One atelier and one standard stand behind the houses. Each royal heritage becomes its own house — drawn from a royal court, living or historical, held to one codex, and offered across three tiers.

The Model

One atelier builds. The houses wear the crown.

Like the great luxury groups, one atelier stands as parent to them all — but unlike them, it makes everything itself. From a house’s first sketch to the finished object, production is one hundred per cent in-house, under one set of quality checks, with lifetime maintenance after.

Each royal house is an independent brand — its own designs, its own philosophy. The atelier carries them to reality and stands behind every piece, but never signs it. Every house is offered at three levels: Sovereign, Flagship and Signature.
