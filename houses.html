Houses Royales

A house for every crown.

TRALH is the atelier and the standard behind the houses. Each royal heritage becomes its own maison — drawn from a living court, held to one codex, and offered across three tiers.

The Model

TRALH builds. The houses wear the crown.

The parent house holds the savoir-faire, the codex, the provenance, and the quality bench — and never signs the product. Each maison carries the identity of a single royal heritage; TRALH gives every one the same uncompromising hand.

Fifteen Maisons

A maison for each living court.

Each is its own world — name, codes, and palette drawn from one royal house; all produced by TRALH. Names are authentic to each court, never the family's own.

House of Al Nahyan · Abu Dhabi

Liwa

Desert-modern luxury — the falcon, sadu geometry, and the gold-thread bisht, tailored sharp.

House of Al Maktoum · Dubai

Shindagha

Maritime gold — pearl, dhow and the modern majlis, in a sea-and-sand palette.

House of Saud · Diriyah

Diriyah

Najdi grandeur — the gold-bordered bisht, mud-fort geometry, and the Ardah in motion.

House of Al Thani · Qatar

Zubarah

Pearl-and-falcon minimalism — museum-grade modern luxury from the Gulf coast.

House of Al Said · Oman
