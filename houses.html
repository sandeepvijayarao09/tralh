Houses Royales

A house for every crown.

TRALH is the atelier and the standard behind the houses. Each royal heritage becomes its own maison — drawn from a living court, held to one codex, and offered across three tiers.

The Model

TRALH builds. The houses wear the crown.

Like the great luxury groups, TRALH is the parent — but it also makes everything. From a house’s first sketch to the finished object, production is one hundred per cent in-house, under TRALH’s own quality checks, with lifetime maintenance after.

Each royal house is an independent brand — its own designs, its own philosophy. TRALH carries them to reality and stands behind every piece, but never signs it. Every house is offered at three levels: Sovereign, Flagship and Signature.
